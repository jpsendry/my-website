Turkey On-The-Clock

Top Regions to Consider


Southeastern Anatolia Region (Şanlıurfa, Malatya, Diyarbakır, etc.)

The farming gem of Turkey. The severe drought and soils malnourished nutrients have devastated agriculture for the farmers here.

Central Anatolia Region (Ankara, Aksaray, Yozgat etc.)

This region heavily relies on steady rainfall to keep up with its large production of barley and wheat. The inconsistent irrigation during the drought has only made matters worse for locals.

Istanbul (Turkish City)

The population within Istanbul has almost 5 times more people than Turkey’s next densest city; Turkey's Capitol, Ankara. The current dam levels being at an all-time low mixing with the rapid growth and infrastructure mismanagement has led to a scarcity of water within this region for many locals and tourists.
