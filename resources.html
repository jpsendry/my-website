Helpful Resources

A safe water system in the Hatay Province of Turkey.

Water Mission is a Christian engineering non-profit that builds sustainable safe water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH) solutions for people in developing countries, refugee camps, and disaster areas. Following the February 2023 Earthquakes in Turkey, Water Mission provided safe drinking water to those impacted. Water Mission installed safe water systems in camps, clinics, and kitchens which provided nearly 57,000 people with safe drinking water. Water Mission’s goal is to solve the global water crisis. To date, Water Mission has served more than 8 million people in 60 countries through 3,000 projects
